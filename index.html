Introduction

HSA is a specialized virtual screening tool designed to predict optimal solubilizers for poorly water-soluble drugs using 3D Hansen Solubility Parameters (HSP).

"Like dissolves like" — By calculating the Ra distance between a drug and excipient, we maximize formulation stability and bioavailability.
