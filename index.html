Welcome to Hansen Solubility Modeling

Hansen Solubility Parameters (HSP) provide a powerful framework for predicting molecular interactions. Our tool allows for precision selection of solubilizers using advanced 3D modeling components: Dispersion (δD), Polar (δP), and Hydrogen Bonding (δH).

This open-source virtual screening tool is designed for academic researchers to find the perfect solvent match for drugs and excipients with unprecedented accuracy[cite: 1, 19].
